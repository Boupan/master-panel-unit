------------LEGAL DISCLAIMER------------------

When dealing with high voltages you should always 
get the help of a certified individual to help you with.

A) Close the mains 
B) Switch on the oven and/or other current-hungry devices to draw any remaining current
C) Make use of the multimeter in voltage sense mode before anything else!!
D) Connect the transformer in an existing secondary line like the bathrooms one that does not
hold any significant devices unless they opperate a dryer or a washing machine in which case
prefer a room's line
E) Then connect the circuit-casing and afterwards connect a second cable from the same line 
through the relay module and into the power relay command line. Finally, connect the existing lines
of the boiler to the power relay by replacing the existing circuit breaker or in parallel to it
